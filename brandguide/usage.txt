Jahatelo brand usage guide

Use icon only
- App icon and adaptive icon
- App splash icon when text is not present
- Small nav or tight spaces

Use icon with Jahatelo text below
- Splash full screen
- Marketing and onboarding screens
- Places where the brand can breathe

Colors
Primary pink and purple from the logo gradient
Text color for Jahatelo in the logo should match the gradient

Notes
Keep backgrounds clean and white for the splash
Avoid extra borders or boxes behind the icon
